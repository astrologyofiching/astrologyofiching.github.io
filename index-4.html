About The Program

Published 1 decade 4 years ago by admin

What do you get?

Working with the program, you get personal birth chart based on I Ching (Chinese Book of Changes) which represents a figured description of forces and influences following you during your lifetime. The recommendations are given in the form of I Ching Hexagrams which are the essence of Chinese Book of Changes. A birth chart is calculated according to the I Ching Astrology system.

Let I Ching help you to analyze your destiny!

Application interface with displayed Birthday Hexagram for Bill Gates (click to enlarge)

It’s as easy as 1, 2, 3…

  1. Type your date and time of birth;
  2. Select your place of birth from the list of cities;
    …and…
  3. Voilà! Done!

As the result you get birthday I Ching Horoscope which includes:

  1. Birthday I Ching Hexagrams:
    1. Pre-Heaven Hexagram. Hexagram which represents forces influencing first part of your life.
    2. Later Heaven Hexagram. Hexagram which represents forces influencing second part of your life.
  2. Yearly I Ching Hexagrams. Series of hexagrams describing influences for each year of life.
  3. Daily I Ching Hexagrams. Series of hexagrams describing influences for each 6-days period in a chosen year.
  4. Yearly Hexagram Statistics. The graph illustrating what life lessons will more often occur during your lifetime.

Every I Ching Hexagram is backed with commentaries which can provide you outstanding visual and intuitive experience. Yearly Hexagrams can help you to understand how you could behave each year to be in harmony with surrounding world. Daily Hexagrams are hints which can help you to “feel” the situation around you and make right decisions every day.

Here are the reasons why Four Pillars of Wisdom can make your destiny analysis AMAZINGLY fascinating:

  1. The program is easy and comfortable to use. No special knowledge of astrology is needed.
  2. Most in-depth among all types of astrology of the ancient China is used in the program.
  3. Several I Ching translations are included in order to make the process of understanding and analyzing hexagrams easier.
  4. The program has a huge database of cities from all over the world, so you will not have to look for coordinates of your place of birth in other sources.
  5. A very comfortable user interface will allow you to “play” with casting horoscopes.
  6. Yearly Hexagram Statistics can help you to understand what “life lessons” will more often occur during your lifetime.
  7. Help and explanations of comments to hexagrams are included. Specially for I Ching beginners!
    …and of course…
  8. If you have a question, at any time of day or year, you can address it to the author!
